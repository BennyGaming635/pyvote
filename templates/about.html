How PyVote Works

PyVote is a LAN-based voting application that allows users to create polls, vote, and view results in real-time. Hereâ€™s how you can use it:

  • Create a Poll: Go to the homepage and enter your question along with options. Submit your poll to make it available to everyone on the network.
  • Vote: Users can see all polls and vote for their preferred option.
  • View Results: Real-time results are displayed on the same page as the polls.

The app works on local networks, making it ideal for classrooms, meetings, or small-scale events.
